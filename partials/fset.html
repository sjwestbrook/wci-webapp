What is FSET

The FoodShare Employment & Training (FSET) program is a no-cost program that helps FoodShare recipients build their job skills and find employment. Adults needing to meet the work requirement can participate in FSET to continue to receive FoodShare benefits.

Additional Information

Workforce Connections, Inc. will assist you in connecting with this program in your local area. For more information call 800-742-5627.
