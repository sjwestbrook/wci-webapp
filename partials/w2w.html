What is Windows to Work

Windows to Work promotes self-sufficiency for individuals returning to the community through skill development and cognitive behavioral training. Services are available within the following correctional facilities in western Wisconsin:

  • Jackson Correctional Institutions (JCI)
  • New Lisbon Correctional Institutions (NLCI)

Additional Information

Participants must be incarcerated at a participating institution and be within 12 months of release. For more information inform your institution social worker or contact Workforce Connections, Inc. at 608-790-8137 800-742-5627.

Program Flyer

Windows to Work program serving the Western Wisconsin region is funded by the Western Wisconsin Workforce Development Board, Inc. under State of Wisconsin Department of Corrections Becky Young Recidivism Reduction Appropriation.
