What is Emergency Assistance

Emergency Assistance provides assistance with establishing permanent housing for persons with minor children who are facing homelessness, have an eviction or foreclosure notice or who have experienced a flood, fire, or other natural disaster. Services are available in Pepin, Buffalo, Trempealeau, Jackson, La Crosse, Monroe, Vernon and Crawford counties. For more information contact Workforce Connections, Inc. at 855-418-9332.
