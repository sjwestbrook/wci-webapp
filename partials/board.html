Kevin Zagrodnick

Kevin Zagrodnick is a Commercial Loan Officer for River Bank and has provided loans and services to businesses of all sizes for 15 years. Kevin graduated from UW-Whitewater with a Mathematics degree. Kevin has been married to his wife, Jeri, for 17 years, has two boys, Eli (13) and Jacob (11), and is the reluctant owner of a dog, Cooper. In his free time he enjoys being active; boating, biking, running, and watching sports are some of his hobbies. He has participated in several marathons and triathlons and completed a full Ironman.

Secretary/Treasurer

Kurt Bear

Kurt Bear, Business Developer for Mid-City Steel, is an accomplished Executive with both domestic and international experience in metal fabrication and construction management. He is responsible for business growth, team development, and strategic thinking and has demonstrated success in market development, and has a track record of improved safety. Kurt has served on the Workforce Connections, Inc. Board of Directors since 2007. In addition, Kurt is the chairperson of The Upper Mississippi Manufacturing Alliance (TUMMA), serves as Vice Chair for the Mayo Clinic Health System Foundation Board, and is a Board member for Junior Achievement. He is passionate about economic empowerment, education, science and technology and the environment.

Angela Menezes

Angela Menezes is a Human Capital Consultant and Business Partner for Logistics Health. She has over 10 years of human resource experience, is successful in developing and implementing HR business plans across the organization and is skilled in supporting leadership in employee relations. Angela has a Bachelor’s Degree and Masters of Business Administration (MBA) from Viterbo University. In addition to being a board member for Workforce Connections, Angela holds professional certifications with the Senior Professional in Human Resource Certification (SPHR), is a LASHRM Member, and belongs to the La Crosse Jaycee’s.
