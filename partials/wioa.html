Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA)

What is Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA)

WIOA provides Adults and Dislocated Workers access to support and services to help obtain and maintain full time employment on a career pathway. WIOA can help you explore job opportunities, connect to necessary training, and help build the skills you need to get you started on a career pathway that works for you.

WIOA is a voluntary program and enrollment is not guaranteed. To be enrolled in WIOA your goals must be realistic and compatible with the program goals. Enrollment does not ensure that all your financial needs will be fully covered.

WIOA Overview

The Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA) Program provides individualized, employment-focused services to job seekers who meet eligibility and program enrollment requirements. The goal is full-time employment, job retention, and sustainable earnings for individuals, thereby improving the quality of the workforce.

WIOA may assist with career planning, job searching, training, and various other needs that may arise in your journey to obtaining employment. WIOA services are free to participant.

Being out of work can be frustrating and an uncertain time. Our staff are trained employment professionals ready to assist you in the journey to employment. Staff can help develop a plan for your search and help equip you with the tools you need for success. Our services are individualized to meet your needs.

WIOA Services

Career Plan

We can guide you to a new job or career. You may have the opportunity to take interest, personality, skills, aptitude, or career assessments to help determine a career path that is right for you.

Job Search

Staff can help you search for positions, develop your resume, improve your interviewing skills, and fill out traditional or online applications. We can also connect you to resources to explore available jobs that are in demand in your local labor market.

Training

Support

Transportation assistance, dependent care assistance, clothing/uniform allowance, medical services, tools, or relocation assistance may be available to help you while in job search activities or training. We are available to answer concerns or questions as you transition into your new job.

Services are individualized to meet your needs. Eligibility requirements may apply.
