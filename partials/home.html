Welcome to Workforce Connections, Inc.!

Since 1983, Workforce Connections, Inc. has assisted job seekers, laid-off workers, and youth with career planning, job search assistance, and skills training.

In addition, employers are assisted with a wide array of services, including recruiting, retaining, and training of employees.
