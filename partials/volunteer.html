Volunteer With Us

Interested in volunteering in your community? Do you enjoy working with children? Workforce Connections, Inc. is recruiting adults 55 and older to become Foster Grandparents. Foster Grandparents are role models, mentors, and friends to children with exceptional needs. The program provides a way for volunteers to stay active by serving children and youth in their communities. Contact Amy Larson at 608-317-9133 or larsona@workforceconnections.org for more information.
